What is BETA at Hooch

Beta at Chattahoochee High School is more than a mere club of volunteers - it's a community; a family of individuals dedicated to consistently provide their community in unique ways. From helping at veterans' associations to making chew toys for pet adoption centers, members of hooch beta have always found ways to give back to their community. Members are held to a high standard and are expected to maintain a culture of academic excellence as well as exemplifying the four pillars of Beta: achievement, character, leadership, and service.
